12-tone equal temperament, and with A at 440Hz, is and has been the standard tuning system for Western music for hundreds of years. While it certainly allows for a huge amount of compositional diversity, it is important in an era where learning music by ear is less and less common to understand different tuning systems and diversify one's sonic palate.
